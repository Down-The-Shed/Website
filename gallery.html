Commission work

This was my first commission of 2024. contacted by Guy Mitson on Etsy, I was asked if I would be able to create two 1m long poles with loops on the end.
After some discussion and rough sketches we decided that 10mm round bar would be the most suitable to avoid deflection.
Having prepared the materials I got to work making them as perfect and identical as I could, even scrapping one attempt.

After finalising the pieces I shipped them off to the customer, a little while after they arrived they sent me the photo on the left to showcase how they were putting them to use to support fireplace clothes dryer.
